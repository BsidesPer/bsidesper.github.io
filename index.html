F.A.Q

  • When do tickets go on sale?

    Ticket sales start 15th May 2019

  • Are children welcome at this event?

    Yes! We encourage people of all ages to get involved with information security! We will even have a space for parents to chill out with their kids, but please note, this is not a baby-sitting service!

  • Do you provide special rates for students?

    Not at this point. If you would really like to come and genuinely can't afford it, please let us know mail@bsidesperth.com.au

  • When does the Call For Presentations open?

    It's open now! Check it out!

  • Can I get a discount if I only want to attend one day of the conference?

    No, we are committed to keeping our $50 ticket price. We believe this is more than reasonable considering the value provided
