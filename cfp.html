Call for Presentations

Calling all speakers for BSides Perth 2019. BSides Perth 2019 is running on the 21st and 22nd of September 2019 at UWA in Perth, Western Australia.

If you’ve never spoken at a con before, this is the con for you! If you’re a seasoned con speaker, this is the con for you! We’re interested in what you have to say, whether you are a veteran of the industry or a new contributor with something you're excited about.

We want to work together as a community to discover new and exciting ideas.

To submit a talk, please fill out this form

Security BSides (commonly referred to as BSides) is a series of loosely affiliated information security conferences. Heavily inspired by BSides Canberra, the BSides Perth team aims to bring that level of professionalism and excitment across the Nullarbor
